## 📌 What is Hair Checkup?

**Hair Checkup** is a product concept addressing a massive gap in personal care: people buy the wrong shampoo every single day. 

It's not about brand loyalty—it's about a lack of guidance. Most consumers choose products based on hype rather than hair chemistry.

---

## 🔍 The Problem

Currently, users choose shampoos based on:
* **Brand Hype** & Marketing
* **Aesthetic Packaging**
* **Discounts** & Price points
* **Influencer Recommendations**

**The Gap:** Almost no one asks: *"Is this scientifically right for MY scalp type?"*

---

## 💡 The Solution

A decision-tree-based recommendation engine that:
1.  **Visually Identifies** hair types via simple UI prompts.
2.  **Diagnoses** specific scalp symptoms (oiliness, dandruff, sensitivity).
3.  **Matches** users with their scientifically "perfect" shampoo.
4.  **Educates** the user on *why* the ingredients work for them.

---

## 👤 Target User

**Demographic:** Adults aged 18–35 in India.  
**Pain Point:** "Serial Switchers" who jump from brand to brand because they never see actual results.
